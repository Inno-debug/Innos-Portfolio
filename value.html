Value Of UXD

Summary

This project explores the UI/UX trends in game development for 2025, focusing on emerging technologies like AI-driven interfaces, AR/VR experiences, and blockchain economy. It also examines the skills required for careers in game product design and data science.

Project Goals

Understand the transformation in the gaming industry

  • Analyze how emerging technologies and evolving player needs are reshaping UI/UX design and development.

Identify core skill requirements

  • Research the technical and soft skills needed for careers in game product design and data science.

Explore career opportunities

  • Investigate job roles and responsibilities in the gaming and insurance data science industries.

List Of Methods We Used

Career Research

Game Product Designer (Game Science)

  • Technical Skills: Web/desktop UI/UX design, improving interaction experience, designing marketing materials.
  • Soft Skills: Cross-team collaboration, strong aesthetics and creativity.

Experimentation Data Scientist

  • Technical Skills: Python, high-level math, advanced statistics, data visualization.
  • Soft Skills: Business savvy, communication, teamwork.

Skill Matching

  • Used Figma to highlight the skills needed in respective industries.

Secondary Research

  • Core Trends in the Game Industry: Startup-like team spirit, developer-driven, player-centric development.
  • Important Trends in Data Science: AI-driven experimentation, real-time data analysis, blockchain for data integrity.

Key Findings

UI/UX Trends in Game Development

  • AI-Driven Dynamic Interfaces: Real-time UI changes based on player behavior, voice/NLP features.
  • AR/VR Immersive Design: Spatial interfaces, gesture controls, balancing immersion with ease of use.
  • Sustainable & Ethical Design: Optimizing energy use, transparent data privacy, accessibility features.

Game Design Trends

  • Blockchain & Decentralized Economy: NFT assets, play-to-earn models.
  • Cloud Gaming & Cross-Platform Connectivity: High-quality visuals on low-end devices, unified saves and social features.
  • Mobile Gaming: Non-gaming apps adding game elements, 5G + AI boosting mobile experiences.

Career Outlook

  • Positive job outlook for video game designers with a 5.31% annual growth rate and 32,090 new jobs expected by 2029.

Personal Contributions

  • Article Selection: Chose articles on AI, gaming UI/UX, game design trends, and the job outlook for video game designers.
  • Skill Matching: Used Figma to compare skills required for data scientists and game developers.
  • Secondary Research: Analyzed trends in the game industry and data science, focusing on emerging technologies and their impact on job roles.
