Value Of UXD

Summary

This project explores the UI/UX trends in game development for 2025, focusing on emerging technologies like AI-driven interfaces, AR/VR experiences, and blockchain economy. It also examines the skills required for careers in game product design and data science.

Project Goals

Understand the transformation in the gaming industry

  • Analyze how emerging technologies and evolving player needs are reshaping UI/UX design and development.

Identify core skill requirements

  • Research the technical and soft skills needed for careers in game product design and data science.

Explore career opportunities

  • Investigate job roles and responsibilities in the gaming and insurance data science industries.

List Of Methods We Used

[Career Research]

  • Game Product Designer (Game Science)

      -Technical Skills

    • Web/desktop UI/UX design, improving interaction experience, designing marketing materials.

      • -Soft Skills

    • Cross-team collaboration, strong aesthetics and creativity.

  • Experimentation Data Scientist

      -Technical Skills

    • Python, high-level math, advanced statistics, data visualization.

      • -Soft Skills

    • Business savvy, communication, teamwork.

[Skill Matching]

  • Used Figma to highlight the skills needed in respective industries.

[Secondary Research]

  • Core Trends in the Game Industry

    • Startup-like team spirit, developer-driven, player-centric development.

  • Important Trends in Data Science

    • AI-driven experimentation, real-time data analysis, blockchain for data integrity.

Key Findings

Research

  • Automated robots face issues like theft, environmental concerns, and worker-related problems
  • Aerial drones are faster but have safety and noise issues

Scenarios and Storyboards

  • Medicine delivery: beneficial but faces security and legal challenges
  • Book delivery: useful but has return strategy and timing issues
  • Mail delivery: good idea but faces accuracy and safety concerns

User Evaluation Results

  • Users found the service useful, especially for book availability and ease of return
  • Feedback for improvements: larger container, more touchscreen functionalities

Personal Contributions

Article Selection

  • Chose an article comparing flying and ground vehicles, learning about their efficiency and theft issues.

Scenarios and Storyboards

  • Analyzed multiple perspectives for medicine, books, and mail delivery.

Sketches

  • Developed base designs and refined them with features like increased security, touch screen, and book dividers.

Final Design and Design Rationale

  • Created a comprehensive design for buying, borrowing, and returning books with enhanced security and usability features.

Overview of Process

  • Conducted interviews, implemented scenarios and storyboards, sketched ideas, tested with users, and made final tweaks.
